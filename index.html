🕋 Umrah Checklist

🛫 Before Leaving

  • Passport, visa & hotel booked
  • Ihram clothes packed
  • Sandals/slippers ready
  • Toiletry bag prepared

📍 At Miqat (Before crossing)

  • Ghusl (shower) or Wudu
  • Wear Ihram clothing
  • Niyyah: “Labbayka Allahumma Umrah”
  • Begin Talbiyah: “Labbayka Allahumma Labbayk…”

🕌 In Makkah

  • Go directly to Masjid al-Haram
  • Continue Talbiyah until Tawaf

🔄 Tawaf

  • Start at Black Stone
  • 7 rounds counterclockwise
  • Men: uncover right shoulder
  • Pray 2 Rak’ahs at Maqam Ibrahim
  • Drink Zamzam water

🔁 Sa’i

  • Start at Safa
  • Walk 7 rounds to/from Marwah
  • Men: jog between green markers

✂️ Hair Trimming

  • Men: shave or trim
  • Women: trim fingertip length

🎉 Completion

  • Remove Ihram
  • Make du‘a and give thanks

May Allah accept your Umrah 🤲
